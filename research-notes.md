# Professional Proposal Design Research

## Key Design Patterns from Professional Templates

### Structure (Multi-page)
- Page 1: Cover page with company branding, project title, client info, date
- Page 2: Executive summary + scope of work
- Page 3: Materials/equipment + timeline
- Page 4: Investment summary with chart + terms + signatures

### Visual Design Elements
- Strong branded header with company colors
- Clean typography with clear hierarchy (large titles, medium subtitles, small body)
- Colored accent bars and dividers between sections
- Tables with alternating row colors for line items
- Prominent pricing table with clear totals
- Doughnut/pie chart for cost breakdown
- Professional footer with page numbers
- Signature lines at the bottom
- Icons next to section headers
- Card-style info boxes with borders
- Subtle background colors for sections

### Color Schemes
- Primary: Deep blue (#1e3a5f or #2563eb)
- Accent: Orange (#f97316) or teal (#0d9488)
- Background: Light gray (#f8f9fa) for alternating sections
- Text: Dark (#1a1a1a) for body, white for headers on dark bg

### Typography
- Headings: Bold, larger (18-24px for titles, 14-16px for sections)
- Body: Regular weight, 10-11px
- Line items: 9-10px in tables
- Use font weight contrast (bold labels, regular values)

### Key Differences from Current Implementation
1. MULTI-PAGE is expected - 1 page is too cramped
2. Cover page is essential for professionalism
3. Line-item pricing table (not just totals)
4. Signature area at the end
5. Page numbers in footer
6. More whitespace - don't cram everything
7. Professional tables with headers and borders
